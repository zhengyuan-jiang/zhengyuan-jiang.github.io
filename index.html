Zhengyuan Jiang

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Duke University, advised by Prof. Neil Gong. I received my B.Eng. from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2022. In summer 2021, I worked with Prof. Yu Li at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I am currently a research scientist intern at ByteDance, focusing on multimodal LLM and AI agent security. I am open to research collaborations—if these directions resonate with you, please feel free to reach out~
